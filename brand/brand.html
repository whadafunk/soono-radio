06 — Usage

Dos & Don'ts

These rules protect legibility and brand consistency across all placements.

Do
Use approved variants only
Brand teal on dark, white on dark, deep teal on light. No other color combinations.
Don't
Don't place on busy backgrounds
The haze gradient requires a flat, dark or light background to read correctly. Avoid photos, gradients, or patterns.
Do
Scale uniformly
Always scale both the icon and wordmark together as a unit. The icon-to-text ratio is fixed.
Don't
Don't recolor the icon independently
Never apply a different color to the icon while keeping the wordmark in another color — both must use the same value.
Do
Use the icon alone at small sizes
Below 120px wide, drop the wordmark and use the icon mark only (favicons, app icons, avatar thumbnails).
Don't
Don't stretch, rotate, or add effects
No drop shadows, outlines, gradients applied to the logo, rotation, or non-uniform scaling.
